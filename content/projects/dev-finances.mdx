# Dev.Finances 

Project developed to better manage earnings and expenses. Project carried out at the Discover @Rocketseat Marathon.

Functionalities

- Website to manage earnings and expenses (financial control)
- Registration and deletion of transactions
- Visualization of the incoming and outgoing balance.
- floating button
- custom scroll bar
- Error Toast - (Modal)
- Color change on 'total' Card - (+$0.00 green ↑ and -$0.01 red ↓)
